Why I built this

My friends and family have joked for years about my habit of turning everyday moments into teaching moments and sprinkling positivity wherever I go. At some point I stopped apologizing for it. Everyone deserves a little something to help swing their day their way, and I started thinking about how to put that out into the world in a simple, accessible way.

Daily Momentum was born from that idea. A positive start creates energy. Energy creates momentum. And momentum, even small amounts of it, can carry you further than you expected when you woke up.

What I believe about people

I believe that understanding a person, motivating them, and encouraging growth inside and outside of their career builds the kind of relationship where real potential gets unlocked. Everyone has potential. Not everyone has had someone in their corner who knows how to help them find it.

I love seeing people go beyond what they believed was possible for themselves. That is the whole point, whether it happens in a one-on-one at work or through a quote on a website someone reads before their morning coffee.

I have spent over 15 years in Customer Success and Support leadership with one consistent belief: take care of your customers, take care of your team, and the numbers follow.
